What is Responsible Consumption and Production?

Responsible Consumption and Production is one of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) established by the United Nations. The aim is to decouple economic growth from environmental degradation by promoting sustainable practices across the entire lifecycle of products and services, from production to consumption to disposal.

Fast fashion is an example of Responsible Consumption and Production, highlighting the misconduct faced, whether its overconsumption of products or the deteriorating work conditions.Fast Fashion prioritises quantity over quality, resulting in lower-quality garments that have a shorter lifespan. Due to this misconduct, low-cost labor are being relied on in lower countries, where workers face poor working conditions, low wages, and lack of labor rights. Violating principles of responsible production, which prioritises fair labor practices and workers rights.
